**Problem: **there is a **gender mismatch**:
 • __hic __is **masculine**,
 • __puella __is **feminine**.

In Classical Latin, agreement in gender is expected.
Therefore, __hic __would normally be **haec** (feminine nominative singular) to agree with __puella__.
